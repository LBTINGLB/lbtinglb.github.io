Concepts of OpenVolumeMesh

  • Incidence Relation:
    • (Intrinsic) top-down incidences and
      • Go from cube->face->edge->vertices
    • bottom-up incidences
      • vise versa
      • go from vertex to half-edge to half-face
      • when adding/deleting entity, the incidence relation would be deleted.
