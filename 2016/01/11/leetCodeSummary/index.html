    1. Median of two sorted Arrays
      • O(log(min(m,n)))
      • Odd case is more important then the even case. (5/2 = 2. But the middle is 3. You’d better use (lenA +lenB +1)/2 to calculate the middle)
      • do not abuse using size_t!!!!!
        • because size_t - int is unpredictable!!!
        • be carefull with type cast.
      • O(m+n) Solution
        • use an idea like merge sort. 2 pointer pA and pB, search through both two lists.
      • O(log(m+n)) Solution
